### FAQ
- **The map shows no nearby restrooms**: This likely means the database has no restroom records near your location. You may need to visit a nearby store or any place that looks like it might have a restroom.

- **Submit a new restroom**: Go to [Submit new restroom](https://www.refugerestrooms.org/restrooms/new)

- **Restroom information is incorrect**: Click *Report incorrect informations* in the expanded restroom detail card, correct the information, and submit it. After that, wait for verification. **Restroom Finder App does not guarantee verification time.**
